Privacy Policy

Old School Pocket Notebook — developed by DrivenAI.ai
Effective date: July 7, 2026

The short version: Old School Pocket Notebook does not collect, transmit, or have access to any of your data. Everything you write stays on your device. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and the app cannot connect to the internet — it does not even request that permission.

1. Who we are

Old School Pocket Notebook is developed by DrivenAI.ai ("we," "us," "our"). This policy explains what happens to your information when you use the app.

2. What data the app collects

None. Specifically:

  • The app does not require or offer account creation, sign-in, or any form of identity verification.
  • The app contains no analytics, telemetry, crash-reporting, or advertising software development kits (SDKs) of any kind.
  • The app makes no network requests. The released (Play Store) version of the app does not even hold the Android INTERNET permission — it is technically incapable of sending data anywhere, not merely configured not to.
  • We — DrivenAI.ai — never receive, see, store, or process any content you create in the app (notebook text, drawings, photos, titles, or anything else). There is no server for it to go to.

3. Where your data lives

Everything you create — notebooks, pages, typed text, drawings, inserted photos, and app settings — is stored locally on your device only, using Android's standard on-device app storage. It is not synced, backed up, or transmitted anywhere automatically.

4. Features that involve your choice to share data

The app includes optional features that let you decide to move your own data off your device. In every case, the destination is chosen by you through Android's built-in system dialogs, and DrivenAI.ai never receives a copy:

  • Share a page — hands an image or text snapshot of a page to the standard Android share sheet, so you can send it to any app you choose (Messages, email, etc.).
  • Export a notebook backup (.osnb file) or a PDF — you pick a destination folder on your device, or the share sheet, via Android's Storage Access Framework. The file goes exactly where you tell it to go.
  • Automatic backup (premium feature) — writes a dated backup file to a folder on your device that you selected once in Settings. The file never leaves your device unless you separately move or share it.
  • Import / restore a backup — reads a .osnb file you select and loads it into the app, on-device.

If you choose to send a shared file or backup to a third-party service (for example, saving it into your own Google Drive, or emailing it), that service's own privacy policy governs how it handles that file — DrivenAI.ai has no visibility into or control over that.

5. Camera and photo permissions

If you use the "Insert photo" feature, the app requests camera access (to take a new photo) or uses the Android system photo picker (to choose an existing one). A photo you insert is copied into the app's private, on-device storage for display on your notebook page. It is never transmitted anywhere by the app.

6. Children's privacy

Old School Pocket Notebook is a general-purpose note-taking app, not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone — regardless of age — because the app does not collect information from anyone at all.

7. Your rights (GDPR / CCPA and similar laws)

Regulations like the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) give you rights over personal data that a company holds about you — the right to access it, correct it, delete it, or export it. Because DrivenAI.ai never receives or stores any of your notebook data or personal information on any server, there is nothing on our end to access, correct, delete, or export on your behalf: your data exists solely on your own device (and anywhere you personally choose to export or share it to). You are always in complete control of it — you can delete any notebook, page, or backup directly in the app, or remove all app data at once by uninstalling the app.

8. Third-party services

The app is distributed through Google Play, which has its own data practices governed by Google's Privacy Policy. If you purchase the premium upgrade, that one-time transaction is processed entirely by Google Play Billing — DrivenAI.ai does not receive or store your payment information.

9. Data retention and deletion

Your data is retained on your device for as long as you keep the app installed and choose to keep it. Deleting a notebook or page inside the app removes it immediately from device storage. Uninstalling the app removes all remaining app data from your device.

10. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the "Effective date" above and post the revised policy at this same address. Because the app does not collect contact information from you, we cannot notify you directly — please check back periodically if you have concerns.
